About Me

I'm a photographer and artist based in Oahu, Hawaii, originally from California. I've always been drawn to the outdoors and find so much inspiration in nature—the light, the waves, and the way people connect in these spaces.

I've had a passion for art for as long as I can remember. I grew up with a sketchbook in my hand and a disposable camera in my pocket, and that love for capturing moments has stayed with me ever since.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me with my family—my husband (a helicopter pilot) and our golden retriever, Rotor. I love surfing, hiking, and just hanging out with my boys.

If you have any questions, please reach out to me!
