The Process

  1. Post Inquiry

    Reach out and we'll get on a Zoom call to chat about your vision and find a date that works.

  2. The Proposal

    You'll receive a custom proposal, contract, and invoice. Once the retainer is paid, your date is reserved.

  3. Location and Style

    I'll help with location ideas and send a questionnaire so we can talk through your vision, outfits, and any questions.

  4. The Shoot & After

    We'll have a relaxed, fun session. You'll receive your gallery via Pic-Time within 2–3 weeks, with options to download and order prints.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you travel?

Yes! I'm happy to travel for sessions. I've shot internationally and love exploring new places with my clients.

How and when will we receive our photos?

You'll receive your gallery through Pic-Time within 2–3 weeks of your session. From there you can download high-resolution images and order prints.

Do you photograph weddings?

I'm passionate about capturing weddings and would love to build my wedding portfolio. I haven't shot one professionally yet, but I'm eager to—reach out if you're interested!
